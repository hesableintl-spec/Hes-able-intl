ORIGIN

Our church began with a vision to create a place where people could experience the love and power of Jesus Christ in a real and transformative way. It was founded by a group of passionate believers who felt called to bring the message of hope, faith, and salvation to their community. From humble beginnings, meeting in small homes and local halls, the church has grown through the dedication of its members, prayer, and God’s guidance. Over the years, we have expanded our reach, built a place of worship, and established programs for youth, families, and the wider community. The foundation of our church is rooted in the desire to glorify God, teach His Word, and serve others. Every step of our journey reflects our commitment to follow Christ faithfully, nurture spiritual growth, and impact lives through acts of love and service. We are proud of our history, grateful for God’s faithfulness, and excited about the future as we continue to grow in faith, hope, and community.

WHO WE ARE

We are a community of believers committed to following Jesus Christ and sharing His love with the world. As a church, we are more than just a building—we are a family, united by faith, hope, and service. Our mission is to glorify God through worship, discipleship, and outreach, while creating a welcoming environment where everyone can grow spiritually, emotionally, and socially. At the heart of who we are is a passion for connecting people to God and to one another. We believe that every individual has a purpose and a unique role in God’s plan, and we seek to help each person discover and fulfill that calling. Our church is a place where lives are transformed through the Word of God, prayer, and fellowship.

OUR BELIEFS

The Bible is God’s Word We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. It is our guide for faith, morals, and daily living. God is One We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit. Jesus Christ is Lord We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully divine and fully human, who came to earth, died on the cross for our sins, rose from the dead, and ascended into heaven. Salvation is only through faith in Him. The Holy Spirit We believe the Holy Spirit empowers believers to live godly lives, provides spiritual gifts, guides us into truth, and comforts and strengthens us in all circumstances. Salvation by Grace through Faith We believe that salvation is a gift from God, received by grace through faith in Jesus Christ—not by works. The Church as God’s Family We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship God, encourage one another, and serve the community. Every member has a role in fulfilling God’s mission on earth. The Power of Prayer We believe prayer is a vital connection to God. Through prayer, we experience guidance, healing, and transformation. The Second Coming of Christ We believe that Jesus Christ will return to judge the living and the dead and establish His eternal kingdom.
